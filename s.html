What is science?

We're surrounded by matter.

It behaves in mysterious ways, governed by laws we don't fully understand.

But our understanding is improving. And in the process, we're learning to do things our ancestors could only have dreamed of.

To do this, we create fallible explanatory theories, test them against physical reality, and iterate based on feedback.

We call this process science. And as far as we know, science has no upper bound.

There's no physical law that prevents us from solving whatever material problems are on the horizon.
