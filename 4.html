What moves art forward?

Nothing lasts forever.

We live in constant flux. The present is always changing, and as a result, so are the demands of the future.

To fulfil these demands, we need to align our short-, medium-, and long-term interests in a single direction. And to do this, we need a non-contradictory story that justifies the effort.

Art moves forward when we test such stories against the world, and adapt them accordingly.

Like science, art is vulnerable to corruption by short-term incentive structures.

But corruption is weak. Nobody, no matter how fast, can outrun reality.
