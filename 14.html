What Makes A Storyteller?

Incentives run the world.

Just like nature, the more contact we have with human nature, the more we learn.

Our ability to understand and shape culture (however local) is formed by dealing with people who are incentivised to act truthfully. Just like with engineering, the less diluted this is, the better.

In the short term, direct contact with human nature is extremely painful.

But maybe the best stories come from the most painful experiences.
